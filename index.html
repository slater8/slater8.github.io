Educating a Divided Society

Our goal is to promote the use of education and open conversation to create a racially inclusive society. Racism cannot be eliminated overnight, but we can take steps now to improve our future.

Podcast: Talking About Diversity

Link: Four students discuss their experiences in several different communities based on their racial background, and the changes in dynamic that occur.

Most of us see racism on a larger scale, but tend to ignore the more subtle actions, comments, and perceptions that accumulate to cause these more salient conflicts. As a mixed race woman who presents and identifies as white, my experience has been different than my fully white peers in subtle ways. Addressing these smaller issues is a first step we can take to educate others and observe our own behavior.
